Q&A

When is the deadline for RSVP-ing?
Please RSVP on or before April 1, 2024.
Are there parking spaces in the venues indicated?
Yes there is enough for the planned guests for the wedding. But remember, less cars = less carbon footprint (and cheaper!)
Are the venues indoor or outdoor?
Welcome dinner is outdoors. Wedding Ceremony is indoors, sadly no AC but fans will be provided. (and there's a giant ceiling fan!). Wedding reception is outdoors and can be covered depending on the weather.
What is the weather in La Union/the PH during the event?
The weather in the PH is hot and humid during June. Ocean temperatures may also shock you as it can be warmer than usual. To cool down, we advise you to wear something comfortable and always drink water.
How can I be sustainable during this trip?
Better to travel in groups to lessen emission. If possible, at least one car should have 4 people in it. If you prefer not to bring a car, let us know so we can introduce you to our friends/families who might have an extra space.
Bring your own water bottle and ask restaurants/accommodations to refill it.
Prioritize eating/buying from local establishments especially those in La Union.
Bring homemade snacks during your travel.
Say No to food waste. Try tasting the food first before you grab more.
I have questions for the groom and bride, where can I contact them?
