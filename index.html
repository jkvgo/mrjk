About Us

Just a small time couple that has developed a passion for enjoying good liquor (responsibly) and wants to share with our fellow islanders.

Sharing the brands that we personally enjoy.

Everything you find in this site has personally gone through our tastebuds and livers. We share them in the hopes that you would enjoy them too. From spirits imported from outside the Philippines to locally fermented craft beers & distilled spirits; we try them all and share the ones we like and we find interesting. While we don't have that much local spirits right now; promoting local is one of our key goals so we are still looking into finding more and posting here.
We also plan to make our own liquor so watch out for that!
