What to Wear

Men

Entourage: Long-sleeves white Barong with black trousers.

Others: Generally Barongs are preferred but anything formal works!

Women

Any colorful long dress/long gown.

Or you can choose from this color palette if you don't know what to get!

Changing

The reception, while still within hotel premises, is going to be on a grassy area by the beach.
We have full access to run around the grass and walk by the beach.

So we understand if you want to change into something more comfortable for the outdoors;
but please try to match with the same theme as mentioned above. :)


The weather in the PH is hot and humid that time of the year so take that into consideration and wear something breathable!
